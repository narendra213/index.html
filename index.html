MS DHONI

INTERNATIONAL CRICKETER

Nick Name : THALA

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer. He is a right handed batter and a wicket-keeper.
Dhoni has captained the most international matches and is the most successful Indian captain.
He has led India to victory in the 2011 Cricket World Cup, the 2007 ICC World Twenty20 and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy,
the only captain to win three different limited overs tournaments.

Dhoni's calm demeanour, exceptional decision-making skills, and ability to lead his team to victory have
made him a great leader and revered figure in the world of sports.
His leadership style holds invaluable lessons for us that transcend the boundaries of the cricket field.
